Our Teas

Da Hong Pao New!

One of the most famous oolong teas, and for good reason. This tea is prized in China for its uniquely prominent mineral taste, and a rich, malty, roasted flavour. Perfect for the colder seasons!

Phoenix Honey Orchid

A dancong tea from Phoenix mountain, this tea is bursting with sweet stone fruit flavours. An ideal match for beginners and experienced drinkers alike, being both approachably sweet and rewardingly complex.

Roasted Tie Guan Yin

A comforting old favourite from Anxi County, this tea is full of sweet, buttery, roasted nut flavours. Gentle on the stomach, easy on the taste buds, this tea seems to fill your entire body with warmth.

Genmaicha

A Japanese tea famous for its use of puffed rice to give the tea a rich, sweet, nutty quality. Matched perfectly with the steamed Japanese green tea leaves, a wonderful dessert tea.

Biluochun Traditional

A full, strong green tea, with a powerful hit of fresh spring grass and a refreshing bitterness. This tea is not for everyone, but for those who want a cooling, deep green tea, this could become a personal favourite.

Dragonwell

The most celebrated and vaunted Chinese green tea, Dragonwell or Longjing is the peak of what most people value in green tea; delicate, full of light spring green flavours and floral notes, skillfully wok-roasted and calming head to toe. Irreplacable!

Silver Needle New!

A white tea made exclusively from the youngest fresh tips of the tea bush. It experiences the least amount of process from leaf to cup, and as a result is one of the gentlest, purest tastes tea can offer. Sweet, soft, and deeply inviting.

White Peony

Full, delicate leaves picked at the earliest spring picking, this tea is syrupy sweet and incredibly floral. A clean taste that will make you want to take long peaceful breaths.

Earl Grey Blend

A blend of punchy aged white tea and aromatic herbs, our Earl Grey Blend is a unique mix of deep, aged fruity flavours and perfumed elegance. A lighter, buttery analogue to the usual Earl Grey Black.

Camomile

A deeply soothing tea, and commonly drank before bed as a caffeine-free and soporific tisane, we find camomile a perfect choice for a truly slow, peaceful afternoon.

Rosehip

A tea that is every bit as full, fruity, and exciting as its vibrant colour! There is not much to be said about this tea that isn't better learned drinking it. A truly delicious and sweet fruit brew.

Rooibos with Lemongrass

A blend of the sweeter, less tannic malt of rooibos tea with the bright and refreshing notes of lemongrass. A more complex and carefully curated herbal blend, we are proud of the unique flavour created here.
