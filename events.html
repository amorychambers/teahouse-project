Homespun - Crafts Exhibit/Market

This weekend our events space is stacked with the most beautiful handmade goods from Homespun Welsh Wool, a Carmarthen based company that is preserving the history tradition of clothing manufacture by taking the wool all the way from sheep to socks. Bethan from Homespun will be giving a short talk on the rich history of the process from 13:00 - 13:45 on Saturday and Sunday. Everything laid out on the table is for sale at a kind discount from Homespun, so please join us to learn about this preserved piece of history and pick up something wonderful to wear.

Tea Mastery - A Visit from Tano Naoki

A rare chance to experience tea brewed and served by an experienced master. Tano Naoki's family have been in the tea trade for four generations, and have been serving tea to guests for more than three decades. We are very grateful for the chance to experience the beauty, history, and serenity of the traditional Japanese tea ceremony with a passionate master of the art.
